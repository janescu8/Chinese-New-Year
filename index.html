Chinese New Year

by Jane, 2022


Lunar new year is a main tradition in Chinese culture. However, during the pandemic, we are constrain from going out to paint the town red or visiting too much of your friends and cousins. People living abroad might as well find it hard to come back for family reunion. What can we do in Lunar new year in 2022?

Clean the house

After the whole year past, you might want a big clean up for your house, you can work with your family, sweep away the dust, wipe the window, or maybe change the style in your house. Then, you can have a neat comfortable house just like a new one.

Buy new clothes

Take this year for example, in department stores or clothes shop, there aren’t just lunar new year discounts. It is also end of the season, so a lot of brands are on sale. You can get new clothes at a low price. In the new coming year, you would have a new appearance in your work place, in your school.

Family reunion dinner

In the lunar new year eve, there are some symbols for certain food that people belief with give them luck in the new year. Like, people belief if you eat longevity noodles you would live longer. Fish is very popular on the dinning table as well. The fish is served whole, with the head and tail, representing a good beginning and ending for the new year to come. Also, people usually won’t eat the whole fish because they belief it means you would have abundant food, wealth, money left for the next year.

There are many more you can do during the lunar new year vacation, you can visit tourist spots when there aren’t a lot of people, take away good food from nice restaurants, call your friends and cousins to wish them good luck. Happy lunar new year!
